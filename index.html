Tea, next to water is the cheapest beverage humans consume.
Drinking the beverage tea has been considered a health-promoting habit since ancient times.
The modern medicinal research is providing a scientific basis for this belief.

Varities Of Tea

  • Herbal Tea
  • Masala Tea
  • Green Tea

1. Herbal Tea

Herbal teas and beverages are derivatives of plants which possess biological activity,
and have been used by humans for centuries for the sustenance of life and livelihood.

2. Masala Tea

Many spices are used in the making of this beverage,
commonly called masala chai, with the most prominent one being ginger.

3. Green Tea

The health benefits of green tea for a wide variety of ailments,
including different types of cancer, heart disease, and liver disease, were reported.
Many of these beneficial effects of green tea are related to its catechin,
particularly (-)-epigallocatechin-3-gallate, content.
